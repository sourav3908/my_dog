My dear dog Mr. Chase !

"The one who stayed with me till the End"

I still remeber the moment why we named him Mr.Chase. That little life we brought from shelter in a white tuxedo suit, who chased me endlessly and even when he ran out of energy.
The one who came running into me "derpingly" no matter time and place. The one whose eyes shine so dazzingly bright that you might get blind. Ha! I remember him being scolded by my mom when he redesigned out sofa. And when he took u-turn moment he stepped on snow on that winter morning. And when he took his last breath on my arms when were on the way to ER.

Hope you are with a good family no matter where and still have those eyes full of life and shine. Hoping you are still chasing with those paws that left a print on us. Hoping we get to meet again in another life.
See ya buddy!
